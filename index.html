Our Story

3Adurian is not just a dessert company; we are passionate artisans dedicated to transforming Malaysia's exotic tropical fruits into extraordinary fusion desserts that bridge traditional Southeast Asian flavors with modern culinary innovation.

Specializing in durian, pandan coconut, and cempedak creations, we craft each dessert with meticulous care, introducing the rich heritage and unmatched flavors of Malaysian tropical fruits to discerning palates worldwide.

Towards A Flavorful Journey

Our ultimate goal is to establish 3Adurian as a globally recognized Malaysian brand, showcasing the rich heritage and unmatched quality of Malaysian tropical fruit desserts worldwide.
